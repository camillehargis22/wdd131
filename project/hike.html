Waimea Hike

I was not prepared for this beautiful hike! The waterfall is only about 3/4 miles. What I did not know was that the hike is through a Botanical Garden. I was prepared for a quick, easy walk in my flip flops, not an all day hike! But it was so beautiful, and they have so many types of plants I have never heard of before. And the lizards are fun to watch running around! When you get to the waterfall, you can jump in! They provide life jackets.
