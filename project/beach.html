Beaches

There are a lot beaches to choose from. I mostly stayed at Pupekea Beach because the house were staying at was right on that beach.There are a lot of surfers. A lot of fish! And it goes decently deep pretty quick so a lot of people just play in the waves or snorkel. I kept trying to take pictures under water, but it wasn't working, so I turned my phone around and ended up catching a great picture of myself!! Right next to Pupekea Beach is Shark's Cove, which is a tide pool with thousands of fish and little tiny crabs. It is also a great place to snorkel in. There is a spot where the waves come up and over that is a little deep, but most of it is knee deep.
