Polynesian Cultural Center (PCC)

I didn't know what to expect at the Polynesian Curltural Center, but it is totally worth going to. I learned a lot, like there are 7 main islands: Hawaii, Tahiti, Samoa, New Zealand, Fiji, Tongo, and I don't remember the other one, unless there was only 6. You walk around to each "island" and they have activities and shows to teach about the cultures. Then at the end you can do a buffet that has some amazing food. Then the theater has a show that combines all the islands and their dances together into one amazing story. It's worth at least seeing the show!
